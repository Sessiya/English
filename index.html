Lesson 1: Introduction to Basic English

Greetings

Englishda qanday salomlashish mumkin:

Hello - Salom

Good morning - Xayrli tong

Good evening - Xayrli kech

Introducing Yourself

O'zingizni qanday tanishtirish haqida misollar:

My name is [Your Name]. - Mening ismim [Sizning ismingiz].

I am from [Your Country]. - Men [Sizning mamlakatingiz] danman.

Common Phrases

Ingliz tilidagi asosiy iboralar:

Thank you - Rahmat

Please - Iltimos

Excuse me - Kechirasiz
