So'z Boyligi

  • Salom - Hello
  • Ismim - My name is
  • Qanday - How
  • Yaxshi - Good
  • Hursand - Happy

Grammatika

Amaliyot: Salomlashishda quyidagi ifodalar ishlatiladi:

  • Hi, my name is [Ismingiz].
  • How are you? - I'm good, thank you!

Mashqlar

Quyidagi jumlalarni to'ldiring:

  • Hi, my name is ____. (ismingizni yozing)
  • How are you? - I'm ____.
