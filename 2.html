Amaliyot (Practice)

Quyidagi jumlalarni to'g'ri fe'l bilan to'ldiring.

  • I ____ to the market. (go, has)
  • They ____ a car. (have, is)
  • She ____ very happy. (am, is)
  • We ____ pizza. (eat, are)
